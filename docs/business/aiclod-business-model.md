# AiClod Business Model

## Overview

AiClod is designed as a multi-tenant hiring marketplace and recruiting SaaS platform. The business model should support subscription revenue, usage-based add-ons, premium AI/admin capabilities, and optional marketplace transaction flows.

## Customer Segments

- Employers and recruiting teams
- Staffing and agency partners
- Enterprise hiring organizations with compliance needs
- Candidates as the talent-side marketplace audience

## Revenue Streams

### Subscription Revenue

- Tiered employer subscriptions based on seat count, job posting volume, analytics depth, and integration access.
- Enterprise plans for SSO, advanced admin controls, audit/compliance, and premium support.

### Usage-Based Revenue

- Pay-per-job-post boosts or promotion credits.
- AI-powered add-ons such as resume scoring, matching, and forecasting quotas.
- Messaging, verification, or communication overage pricing where applicable.

### Marketplace and Transaction Revenue

- Featured listing placements.
- Agency referral or placement-fee agreements where legally appropriate.
- Cross-border hiring/compliance services as premium add-ons.

## Pricing Structure

- Free or low-cost starter tier for early employer adoption.
- Growth tier for active recruiting teams with workflow automation.
- Enterprise tier for governance, analytics, advanced integrations, and global compliance support.
- Optional admin/AI feature packs sold as add-ons rather than bundled universally.

## Cost Drivers

- Infrastructure for search, storage, messaging, and observability.
- AI inference, embedding generation, and evaluation costs.
- Fraud review, moderation operations, customer support, and compliance functions.
- Payment processing and international tax/compliance overhead.

## Core KPIs

- monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- annual recurring revenue (ARR)
- gross payment volume (GPV/GMV)
- customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- net revenue retention (NRR)
- time-to-fill and hiring conversion improvements attributable to the platform
- AI feature adoption and cost-to-revenue ratio

## Packaging Recommendations

- Keep foundational hiring workflows in the base subscription.
- Treat AI forecasting, advanced matching, and premium admin controls as higher-tier or metered products.
- Localize pricing, currency, and tax behavior by market while preserving a common product catalog.

## Go-to-Market Notes

- Land with SMB and mid-market employers using fast onboarding and marketplace reach.
- Expand into enterprise via admin governance, analytics, security, and compliance features.
- Use candidate traffic and ecosystem integrations to increase marketplace defensibility over time.
